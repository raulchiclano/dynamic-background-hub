Formal Validation of the Dynamic Background

This laboratory contains the computational core of the DBH. We have transitioned from a “Proof of Concept” to a Formal Validation Suite, where the hypothesis is subjected to rigorous symbolic and numerical testing.

Methodology

Our validation framework operates on two complementary levels:

  1. Symbolic Tensor Calculus: Using SymPy to derive exact field equations, energy-momentum tensors, and the analytical emergence of the MOND scale (\(a_0\)).
  2. Numerical Hydrodynamics: Solving the relativistic Gross-Pitaevskii and FLRW equations using the Split-Step Fourier Method and high-precision ODE integrators.

Key Milestones in v3.5

  • Microphysics: Emergence of spin-1/2 statistics from \(Z_2\) nematic symmetry (Berry Phase \(\pi\)).
  • Gravity: Analytical derivation of the galactic transition scale \(\rho_c = \sigma^2/4\beta^2\).
  • Cosmology: Mapping of the parameter space against Planck 2018 + BAO + SNe constraints.
